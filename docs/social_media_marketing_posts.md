## Posting Strategy Notes

- **TikTok:** Post 3x/week. The "If I Die" binder, Swedish death cleaning, and "what happens when you die" topics have proven viral potential. Lead with a hook question in the first line.
- **Instagram:** Post 2x/week. Carousel format works well for the "two things" (Post 9) and "8 categories" content. Text on a dark background matching the app's colour scheme (deep navy #2D3142, amber #C9963A).
- **Tone:** Serious, direct, never flippant. This is a sensitive topic — respect it. No emojis in the actual post text. Let the gravity of the subject do the work.
- **CTA:** Every post should end with a soft call to action or a link in bio reference. Avoid hard-sell language.
