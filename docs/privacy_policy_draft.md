# Privacy Policy (Draft)

**Last Updated:** 17 March 2026

> **Legal Review**: This document is a draft and must be reviewed by a qualified solicitor or legal counsel before publication. The content is provided for internal planning only and does not constitute legal advice.

At "After Me," we believe that your data belongs to you. We are built on a "Local-First" and "Zero-Knowledge" architecture. This means we cannot see, access, or sell your data because it is encrypted on your device with keys we do not possess.

## 1. Information We Collect
-   **None of your documents**: We do not upload your documents to our servers. They stay on your device or in your personal iCloud (if enabled).
-   **Crash Reports**: We do not collect analytics data. Crash reports are sent to Sentry with personally identifiable information excluded. These reports contain only technical metadata such as device model, OS version, and stack traces. No document content, titles, or personal information is included.
-   **Payment Information**: All transactions are processed by Apple or Google via the App Store or Google Play. We do not store your credit card details.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
-   **To provide the service**: The app functions entirely offline for core features.
-   **To improve the app**: We use crash report metadata to fix bugs and improve stability. No personal information or document content is included in these reports.

## 3. Data Storage & Security
-   **Local Storage**: Your data is stored encrypted on your device using AES-256-GCM encryption. The keys are protected by your device's Secure Enclave (Face ID / Touch ID).
-   **Cloud Backup (Optional)**: If you enable iCloud Backup, your encrypted data is synced to your personal iCloud account. Apple encrypts this data in transit and at rest. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

## 4. Data Retention
-   **Crash Reports**: Crash and error metadata sent to Sentry is retained for 90 days, after which it is automatically deleted.
-   **User Documents**: Your documents are stored locally on your device with no retention limit. You control when and how your data is deleted by removing documents within the app or by deleting the app entirely.

## 5. Third-Party Processors
-   **Sentry** (Functional Software, Inc.): Used for crash reporting only. Sentry receives crash metadata (device model, OS version, stack traces). Personally identifiable information is excluded via PII scrubbing. No document content or user-identifiable data is transmitted. See [Sentry's Privacy Policy](https://sentry.io/privacy/) for details.
-   **Apple / Google**: In-app purchase transactions are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). Their respective privacy policies apply.

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR)
-   **Access**: You have full access to your data within the app.
-   **Deletion**: You can delete your vault at any time by deleting the app. Since we do not store your data on our servers, there is no "account" to delete on our end.
-   **Portability**: You can export your data using the "Family Kit" or "Personal Recovery Kit" features.

## 7. Your Rights (CCPA)
-   **Do Not Sell My Personal Information**: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. We have never sold personal information and have no plans to do so.
-   **Right to Opt Out**: Because we do not sell or share personal information for advertising, there is no sale to opt out of. If this practice ever changes, we will provide a clear opt-out mechanism.
-   **Right to Know**: You may request details about the categories of personal information we collect and the purposes for which it is used. Given our zero-knowledge architecture, the only data we process is anonymised crash metadata via Sentry.
-   **Right to Delete**: You can delete all of your data at any time by removing documents within the app or by deleting the app from your device.

## 8. International Data Transfers
Your documents never leave your device. Crash reports may be processed by Sentry's infrastructure (EU/US). No document content is ever transmitted. Sentry complies with applicable data protection frameworks for international transfers.

## 9. Contact Us
For privacy-related questions, please contact: privacy@afterme.app
