# Privacy Policy (Draft)

**Last Updated:** [Date]

At "After Me," we believe that your data belongs to you. We are built on a "Local-First" and "Zero-Knowledge" architecture. This means we cannot see, access, or sell your data because it is encrypted on your device with keys we do not possess.

## 1. Information We Collect
-   **None of your documents**: We do not upload your documents to our servers. They stay on your device or in your personal iCloud (if enabled).
-   **Device Information**: We may collect anonymous crash reports and performance metrics (e.g., app launch time) via Apple's opt-in analytics. This data never contains personal information or document content.
-   **Payment Information**: All transactions are processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not store your credit card details.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
-   **To provide the service**: The app functions entirely offline for core features.
-   **To improve the app**: We use aggregated, anonymous data to fix bugs and improve performance.

## 3. Data Storage & Security
-   **Local Storage**: Your data is stored encrypted on your device using AES-256-GCM encryption. The keys are protected by your device's Secure Enclave (Face ID / Touch ID).
-   **Cloud Backup (Optional)**: If you enable iCloud Backup, your encrypted data is synced to your personal iCloud account. Apple encrypts this data in transit and at rest. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

## 4. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)
-   **Access**: You have full access to your data within the app.
-   **Deletion**: You can delete your vault at any time by deleting the app. Since we do not store your data on our servers, there is no "account" to delete on our end.
-   **Portability**: You can export your data using the "Family Kit" or "Personal Recovery Kit" features.

## 5. Contact Us
For privacy-related questions, please contact: privacy@afterme.app
