## 4. Brand Consistency Checklist

Use these across both platforms and all content.

### Colours
| Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Deep Navy | `#2D3142` | Backgrounds, text overlays |
| Amber | `#C9963A` | Highlights, CTAs, accent text |
| Off-White | `#FAF9F6` | Body text on dark backgrounds |

### Fonts (for any graphic design / Canva templates)
- **Headlines:** Playfair Display (Bold or Black)
- **Body:** DM Sans (Light or Regular)

### Tone of Voice
- Serious, never flippant
- Direct, never salesy
- Empathetic, never patronising
- Honest about what the app does and doesn't do
- No emojis in post captions (the subject matter is serious)
- Emojis are fine in Stories, Reels overlays, and comments

### Language Rules
- Say "encrypted vault file" not ".afterme file" (lead with the concept)
- Say "QR Key Card" not "QR code" when referring to the printed card
- Say "your family" not "beneficiaries" or "designated contacts"
- Say "vault" not "storage" or "database"
- Say "pay once" not "one-time purchase" or "lifetime subscription"
- Never say "death app" — say "end-of-life planning" or "legacy planning"

## 5. Linked Accounts and Cross-Posting

- Link Instagram and TikTok accounts together in both apps' settings
- Enable "Share to Instagram" when posting TikTok videos (and vice versa)
- Use the same posting schedule:
  - **TikTok:** 3x per week (Tue, Thu, Sat)
  - **Instagram:** 2x per week (Mon, Fri)
  - **Instagram Stories:** 2-3x per week (behind-the-scenes, polls, Q&A)

## 7. Additional Platform Settings

### Instagram
- Turn **on** "Suggest your account to non-followers"
- Turn **on** "Allow sharing to Stories"
- Turn **off** "Hide like and view counts" (social proof helps early)
- Set **Sensitive content control** to "Standard"

### TikTok
- Turn **on** "Suggest your account to others"
- Turn **on** "Allow your videos to be downloaded"
- Turn **on** "Allow Stitch" and "Allow Duet" (engagement signals)
- Set content language to **English (UK)**

## 8. Content Creation Tools

For text-based posts on dark backgrounds matching the brand:

- **Canva** (free) — create templates with the brand colours and fonts
- Template size: 1080x1350px for Instagram feed, 1080x1920px for Stories/TikTok
- Background: #2D3142
- Text: #FAF9F6 (body), #C9963A (highlights/emphasis)
- Font: Playfair Display for headlines, DM Sans for body

Save a brand kit in Canva with these colours and fonts so every post is consistent.
